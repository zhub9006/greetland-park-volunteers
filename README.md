# 🌿 Greetland Community Group — Park Volunteer Outings

Welcome to the official planning hub for the **Greetland Community Group's** volunteer outings to national parks and protected landscapes across **northern England**. This repository tracks everything from park information and upcoming events through to volunteer signups, transport, and supplies.

---

## 🗺️ Northern England National Parks & Landscapes

Greetland sits in the heart of West Yorkshire — we're brilliantly placed to reach several outstanding national parks and AONBs within easy reach. Below is a summary of the best options for group volunteer outings.

---

### 1. 🌿 Nidderdale AONB *(closest to Greetland!)*
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~20 miles (approx. 35 min)
- **Website:** [nidderdaleaonb.org.uk](https://www.nidderdaleaonb.org.uk)
- **Highlights:** Stunning Dales scenery, Brimham Rocks, Stump Cross Caverns, Gouthwaite Reservoir
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Habitat management and wildflower meadow restoration
  - Footpath maintenance
  - Hedgerow planting and management
  - Moorland and river corridor conservation
- **Community Events:** Regular volunteer days — ideal for a shorter, accessible outing from Greetland
- **Contact:** [nidderdaleaonb.org.uk/get-involved](https://www.nidderdaleaonb.org.uk/get-involved)

---

### 2. 🏞️ Peak District National Park
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~25 miles (approx. 45 min)
- **Website:** [peakdistrict.gov.uk](https://www.peakdistrict.gov.uk)
- **Highlights:** Dark Peak gritstone edges, White Peak limestone dales, Kinder Scout, Stanage Edge
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Footpath repair and erosion control on high-traffic routes
  - Moorland and peat restoration
  - Dry-stone walling — a classic Peak District skill
  - Guided walk leadership training
- **Community Events:** Peak District Volunteer Ranger Service holds monthly conservation days
- **Contact:** [peakdistrict.gov.uk/learning-and-volunteering](https://www.peakdistrict.gov.uk/learning-and-volunteering)

---

### 3. 🏔️ Yorkshire Dales National Park
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~40 miles (approx. 1 hour)
- **Website:** [yorkshiredales.org.uk](https://www.yorkshiredales.org.uk)
- **Highlights:** Limestone pavements, traditional dales villages, Ingleborough, Pen-y-ghent, Malham Cove
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Dry-stone wall restoration with the Yorkshire Dales Millennium Trust (YDMT)
  - Path maintenance and erosion repair with Fix the Fells
  - Hay meadow restoration and wildflower surveys
  - Woodland planting and invasive species removal (Himalayan balsam!)
- **Community Events:** Regular volunteer days run by YDMT and the National Park Authority
- **Contact:** [ydmt.org](https://www.ydmt.org) | 01756 748886

---

### 4. 🌲 North York Moors National Park
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~55 miles (approx. 1 hr 10 min)
- **Website:** [northyorkmoors.org.uk](https://www.northyorkmoors.org.uk)
- **Highlights:** Sweeping heather moorland, dramatic sea cliffs, Whitby Abbey, the Cleveland Way
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Moorland management and heather restoration
  - Footpath and bridleway maintenance
  - Coastline and beach clean-up events
  - Biodiversity monitoring (curlews, skylarks, invertebrates)
- **Community Events:** The Moors & Coast Volunteers programme runs regular weekend working parties
- **Contact:** [northyorkmoors.org.uk/get-involved/volunteer](https://www.northyorkmoors.org.uk/get-involved/volunteer)

---

### 5. 🌊 Lake District National Park
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~80 miles (approx. 1 hr 30 min)
- **Website:** [lakedistrict.gov.uk](https://www.lakedistrict.gov.uk)
- **Highlights:** England's largest national park, UNESCO World Heritage Site, dramatic fells and glacial lakes
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Fix the Fells path maintenance on popular upland routes (Helvellyn, Scafell Pike approaches)
  - Lakeshore and woodland conservation
  - Invasive species control (rhododendron, Himalayan balsam)
  - Community archaeology projects
- **Community Events:** Fix the Fells and the National Trust run regular volunteer days
- **Contact:** [fixthefells.co.uk](https://www.fixthefells.co.uk)

---

### 6. 🏰 Northumberland National Park
- **Distance from Greetland:** ~110 miles (approx. 2 hrs)
- **Website:** [northumberlandnationalpark.org.uk](https://www.northumberlandnationalpark.org.uk)
- **Highlights:** England's least-visited national park, Hadrian's Wall, the Cheviot Hills, Gold Tier dark sky reserve
- **Volunteer Opportunities:**
  - Hadrian's Wall path maintenance
  - Peatland restoration
  - Dark sky conservation and astronomy community events
  - Wildlife monitoring (red squirrels, otters, raptors)
- **Community Events:** The Northumberland National Park Authority runs a Volunteer Ranger programme
- **Contact:** [northumberlandnationalpark.org.uk/get-involved](https://www.northumberlandnationalpark.org.uk/get-involved)

---

## 📅 Types of Community Volunteer Events

Here are the most common and rewarding volunteer event types at northern England parks:

| Event Type | Description | Best Parks |
|---|---|---|
| 🪨 Dry-stone walling | Traditional skill, great for groups | Yorkshire Dales, Peak District |
| 🌿 Path maintenance | Erosion repair, drainage, surface restoration | All parks |
| 🌸 Wildflower meadow days | Planting, scything, seed collection | Yorkshire Dales, Nidderdale |
| 🌊 Shoreline/litter clean-ups | Beach and riverside litter removal | North York Moors coast |
| 🌲 Woodland planting | Tree planting, invasive species removal | Lake District, Yorkshire Dales |
| 🐦 Wildlife surveys | Bird counts, bat surveys, invertebrate monitoring | All parks |
| 🏔️ Moorland restoration | Peatland, heather management | North York Moors, Peak District |
| 🥾 Guided walk support | Assisting ranger-led community walks | All parks |
| 🌿 Himalayan balsam bashing | Removing invasive species from riverbanks | Yorkshire Dales, Lake District |
